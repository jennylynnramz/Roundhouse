Roundhouse Track Services currently provides 3 standard off-the-shelf training classes

Two-day Elevated Crane Trackage Inspector Course:
Provides training for inspectors who have to inspect the rails and support structure for top running bridge cranes. Training consists of classroom instruction.
Three Day Crane Trackage Inspector Course:
Provides the same training as the Elevated Training Class above plus includes modules covering ground level trackage for Portal, Container, Gantry and similar weight handling equipment. Training consists of classroom instruction and field exercises.
